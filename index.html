About me

Hi, I’m Swaraj Acharekar — a marketing grad student with a creative soul and an analytical mind. I like to say I speak fluent Gen Z and Excel. My background blends communications, content creation, and data-driven thinking — whether I’m planning a campaign, diving into survey results, or designing social media creatives.

Currently studying Marketing at Yeshiva University, I’ve worked on projects ranging from consumer research on short-form video to web strategy for real-world clients. I love uncovering what makes people tick and turning that into strategy that works — and resonates.

From storytelling to segmentation, I enjoy wearing multiple hats, especially the ones that let me blend creativity, curiosity, and conversion.
